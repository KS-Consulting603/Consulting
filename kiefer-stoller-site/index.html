About

Built on real-world experience.

Kiefer Stoller is an independent accounting and business consultant based in Manchester, NH. After years of hands-on experience — including service in the Marine Corps and a background in technical trades — Kiefer brings a practical, no-nonsense approach to financial clarity for small businesses.

Currently completing a B.S. in Accounting at SNHU and working as a seasonal tax preparer, Kiefer works with business owners who need someone who understands both the numbers and how a real business actually operates.

The practice is small by design — every engagement gets direct attention, not a junior associate.
